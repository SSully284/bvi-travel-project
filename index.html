Why the British Virgin Islands?

Explore warm sands and beautiful reefs

This British Overseas Territory consists of 4 larger islands: Tortola, Anegada, Virgin Gorda, and Jost Van Dyke. There are also 32 smaller islands and islets that make up the British Virgin islands, of which more than 20 are uninhabited. There are 63 popular dive sites in the BVI, which include 57 coral reef sites and 6 artificial reefs that have been created by shipwrecks.

Sailing is one of the favorite sports in the British Virgin Islands. The activity is a popular attraction for tourists who have fun in anything from small boats to fully decked-out and crewed luxury yachts can be rented or chartered. Other water-based activities include island-hopping, poker-run, snorkeling, windsurfing, scuba diving, fishing, and more. With clean pristine beaches and some of the finest sand (Loblolly beach, Anegada) you are sure to make memories you won't forget.

Cafés

My favorite cafés in the BVI

Beans Café

Beans Coffee at Tortola Pier Park serves specialty coffees, teas, smoothies and pastries. They offer a wide variety of coffee and non-coffee drinks for everyone.

Address

Tortola, Pier Park

What I like about it

They open fairly early and have great customer service and tasty drinks every time. They also offer online orders and pick-up for you to enjoy on the go.

French Deli

The French Deli, though it is an actual deli, has a quaint little café presence for those looking to have a seat and eat some delicious pastries.

Address

Road Town, Tortola

What I like about it

They have an actual shop and offer European goods you wouldn't find elsewhere on the island. There's something tasty for everyone.

Island Roots

Island Roots offers everything from a light crispy breakfast with coffee, to a full on breakfast to leave you satisfied. They also have granola and yogurt options.

Address

Main Street, Tortola

What I like about it

They have a cozy vibe you can't get anywhere else, and the food is delicious. For brunch lovers this is a sweet spot for you to hide away.
